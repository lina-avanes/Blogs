Blog 10: Creating A New Group & User In AWS IAM

In todays blog, we will go over how to create groups and users in AWS.

STEP 1:

Sign in to your AWS console and go to Services > IAM.

STEP 2:

Click on Groups.

STEP 3:

Click on "Create New Group".

STEP 4:

Name your group and click on "Next Step".

STEP 5:

Attach a policy to your group.In this example, I will give this group the administratoraccess policy.

STEP 6:

Review

STEP 7:

As we can see the new group "example" has been created and added to the list of our groups.



Creating & Adding A User To A Group

STEP 1:

Click on "Users".

STEP 2:

Click on "Add User".

STEP 3:

Choose a username & select an AWS access type and then click on "Next Permissions".

STEP 4:

Here, we will have to choose a group for our user. We will choose the group created in the first part which is "example". Click on "Next:Tags".

STEP 5:

Tags are optional. No tags will be used in this example and therefore we will click on "Next: Review".

STEP 6:

Review and then click on "Create User".

STEP 7:

Once "Create User" is clicked, we will see the user and the group that it belongs to.
